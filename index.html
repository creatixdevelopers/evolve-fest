About The Evolve Fest

Evolve Fest 2025 is a three-day youth spiritual retreat designed to spark inner transformation, self-discovery, and sacred joy. Held in the serene and high-energy environment of Sri Sidheshwar Tirth - Sri Brahmrishi Ashram, Tirupati, this immersive gathering invites seekers aged 16 to 36 to pause, reflect, and reconnect with their deepest self.

Inspired by the vision of Sri Siddhguruvar, Evolve Fest is not just a festival  - it’s a soulful journey. From powerful guided meditations, interactive soul sessions, and ancient rituals to playful treks, sunrise yoga, and community satsangs, each moment is curated to harmonize the body, mind, and spirit.

Whether you're a curious beginner or a passionate seeker, Evolve Fest 2025 opens a sacred space to:

Awaken your inner silence
Dive into spiritual practices rooted in Vedic wisdom
Experience meaningful connections with like-minded youth
And return home with clarity, energy, and peace

Join us as we awaken, evolve, and empower — together

Meet Sri Siddhguruvar

Sri Siddhguruvar is a living embodiment of divine energy and timeless Vedic wisdom. Through silent grace and unseen transmission, He guides seekers on the path of AatmaVriddhi (inner expansion) and GuruSiddhi (connection with the Master’s divine consciousness)  often without even a physical meeting. Rooted in the lineage of enlightened Himalayan masters, Sri Siddhguruvar awakens the inner light of countless lives through subtle blessings, sacred silence, and powerful spiritual technologies. His presence offers clarity, purpose, healing, and direction to seekers across the world. His vision is manifesting a new generation of awakened youth, those who live with awareness, serve with compassion, and walk the path of dharma in everyday life.

“The world does not need more followers. It needs awakened youth who live with awareness, courage, and compassion.”
Come experience His presence. Not through belief, but through direct inner connection.

About Sri Brahmrishi Ashram

Sri Brahmrishi Ashram is not just a place - it is a living presence. Nestled in the spiritual city of Tirupati, the ashram is a sanctuary for seekers walking the inner path. Founded under the divine vision of Sri Siddhguruvar, the ashram serves as a sacred ground for meditation, self-discovery, Siksha (Education)  and seva (selfless service).

Rooted in ancient Himalayan wisdom and adapted for modern life, the ashram offers powerful tools for spiritual growth  including guided sadhanas, satsangs, and transformative silence retreats. Every corner of the space radiates stillness, inviting each soul to go beyond the noise of the world and meet the divine within.

Come, not just to visit. Come to experience.
